Homesprinter

  • Client

    side project

  • Role

    Website, front-end &
    back-end developer

  • Sector

    Real Estate

Homesprinter is a real estate web application that allows users to search for homes in the Jersey City area by neighborhood or zip code. Users can also sign up to create an account to keep track of saved homes. Listings were curated in late 2018 and have not been updated since.

COLOR

Green contrasted by greys were chosen to reflect a sense of excitement and deliberation often experienced throughout the home search process.
