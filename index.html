ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Thalia and I am a full stack web developer based in Jersey City. I graduated from Rutgers University with a B.A. degree in Computer Science. I enrolled at Rutgers knowing very little about computer science and graduated knowing well...still very little in the grand scheme of things. Like many people in this field I like to problem solve and add some artistic flair when possible. I like completing projects and showing them to less than excited family members and friends. This pushed me to take a deep dive down the rabbit hole that is web development. From creating mobile responsive designs to working on the logic behind the application in MVC let's just say I came out looking like that bunny from Donnie Darko. I can code, use Git, and the drive to keep on learning.
