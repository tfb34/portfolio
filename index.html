ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Thalia and I am a full stack web developer based in Jersey City. I graduated from Rutgers University with a B.A. degree in Computer Science. I enrolled at Rutgers knowing very little about computer science and graduated knowing well...a little bit about a lot of things. Like many people in this field I like to problem solve and add some artistic flair when possible. I like completing projects and showing them to less than excited family members and friends, hence all my game projects below. This pushed me to take a deep dive down the rabbit hole that is web development. From creating mobile responsive designs, to utilizing webpack, and working on the business logic behind the application in MVC, let's just say I had less hair than what I started with. I can code, use Git, and have the drive to keep on learning.
